About me

Hi, I’m Larana - a florist based in Chicago with a lifelong love for flowers and design. I draw inspiration from the natural beauty around me - the changing seasons, city gardens, and everyday moments that deserve to be celebrated.

How it all started?

What began in 2010 as a small creative dream has blossomed into a full-fledged passion - my own floral boutique and fresh flower delivery service right here in Chicago.

Years of experience

With over 15 years of experience, I’ve learned that every bouquet carries more than beauty - it carries meaning. I handpick each stem, thoughtfully arranging every detail to tell a story, capture a feeling, or brighten someone’s day.

From simple beginnings to a beloved local flower studio, my journey has always been rooted in one thing: creating floral art that connects people through nature’s most beautiful language.
