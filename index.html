About me

Hi, I’m Larana - a florist based in Chicago with a lifelong love for flowers and design. I draw inspiration from the natural beauty around me — the changing seasons, city gardens, and everyday moments that deserve to be celebrated.

What started as a small creative dream has grown into my own boutique and fresh flower delivery service right here in Chicago. Every bouquet I make is personal - I handpick each stem and craft every arrangement to tell a story, capture a feeling, or brighten someone’s day.

Whether you’re celebrating, saying thank you, or simply bringing joy to your home, I pour my heart into every creation to make sure it feels genuine, beautiful, and uniquely yours.

My services

Whether you’d like to visit my flower shop in person or order your bouquet online, this is the perfect place to find the arrangement that speaks to you. From classic roses to seasonal garden blooms, I create every piece with care, style, and a personal touch - perfect for any occasion.

Visit my boutique

Step inside my Chicago floral boutique and explore a world of color, fragrance, and inspiration. Here, you can see each bouquet up close, choose your favorite flowers, and even request a custom arrangement on the spot. I love meeting my clients in person and helping them create something truly special.

Order online

Shopping for flowers online has never been easier! Browse my collection of fresh, hand-tied arrangements and seasonal designs - all available for same-day delivery within Chicago. Every order is prepared with the same love and attention to detail as in-store purchases, and delivery is always free across Chicago.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! I offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM within Chicago city limits. Orders placed after 2 PM will be delivered the next day.

Can I customize my bouquet?

Absolutely! I love creating custom arrangements. You can either visit my boutique or contact me online to discuss your preferences and ideas.

What flowers are in season?

The flowers available vary by season. I always use the freshest and most beautiful blooms that are in season to create my arrangements. Feel free to ask me about seasonal options!
